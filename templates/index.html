About

Who we are

Welliva is a user-friendly platform designed to simplify the healthcare experience by making medical reports easy to access and understand. We believe that every individual has the right to be fully informed about their health, without the complexity and confusion that often comes with medical terminology. At Welliva, you can securely access the results of your medical tests and reports anytime, anywhere. But we don’t stop there. We know that medical reports can be overwhelming, filled with complex terms and data that can be difficult to interpret. That’s why we take it a step further by breaking down those terms and explaining them in simple, everyday language. No more guessing or searching the web for definitions—you’ll have clear, concise explanations right alongside your results.

  • Check the results of reports from anywhere.
  • Detail info of the disease which has been found.
  • Graphical representation of the Person's Growth.

Our platform is designed with patients in mind, providing clarity and peace of mind. Whether it’s understanding what a specific medical term means, learning about how certain health metrics impact your overall well-being, or simply gaining insight into your test results, Welliva makes the process easy and stress-free. With Welliva, you are not just receiving your health data—you’re gaining the knowledge and tools to actively participate in your own healthcare journey. It’s healthcare, simplified.
